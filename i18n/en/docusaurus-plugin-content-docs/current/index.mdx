# Gijs Great Makeover

Welcome to the Gijs Great Makeover blog.

This series started as a bit of a joke. Right after we bought our first house, people kept asking how the renovation was going. We had loads of photos, so instead of repeating the same update over and over, I started writing blog posts.

Years later, the series is still alive, still growing, and still full of questionable DIY decisions.
